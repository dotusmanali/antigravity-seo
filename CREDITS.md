## Upstream Lineage

Antigravity SEO is a Antigravity-first port of [`dotusmanali/antigravity-seo`](https://github.com/dotusmanali/antigravity-seo). It preserves the upstream SEO workflows while adapting packaging, agents, plugin metadata, cache paths, installers, and deterministic runners for Antigravity.
